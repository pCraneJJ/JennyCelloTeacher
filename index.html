Jenny Crane is a multi-faceted cellist who is passionate about performing New music. She currently teaches out of Calgary and records music collaborating in any type of genre. She holds a master’s degree in music from the San Francisco Conservatory of Music and an undergraduate degree from the University of Montreal. She has 5 + years of experience as a Cello teacher from all ages - beginner to advanced.

She began her cello studies with John Kadz in Calgary, Alberta, at Mount Royal University’s conservatory program. There, Jenny had the opportunity to learn from mentors such as Andres Diaz, Laurence Lesser, Anton Kuerti, Hans Jorgen-Jensen, Desmond Hoebig, and Ron Leonard. A highlight in her career was performing for the composer Krzystof Penderecki at the Morningside Music bridge international music program in Gdansk Poland, playing movements from his Divertimento for solo Cello. In Calgary she performs with the jazz band Czarkologie and works with music producers, composing and recording music for the Cello across multiple music genres.
