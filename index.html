Your Musical Development

I believe that becoming an accomplished musician is not about the number of hours practice invested but rather the quality of mindful listening guided by a teacher. Often substantial progress may come through small changes in the way we play.
80

Practice

We achieve progress through small daily improvements. They add up and consistency is important so find a time of the day that protected. Perfectionism can be self defeating. Don't get blocked by something you think could be better. A software app like Modacity on your cellphone can guided you make the most of your daily practice.
90

Listen

We can become better musicians by listening to others - and not just other cellists. Listen to singers too.
85

Perform

We have all been through this. We stand before our audience; place our music which we have been practising everyday for the past six months on our music stand; look out at our audience; look down at our music and we could swear we've never seen this music before. Play for others at every opportunity. We develop confidence everytime we perform.
